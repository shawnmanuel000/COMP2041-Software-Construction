The panel shows the details that the class account maintains about you as a student. The class (including year and session) is listed under ClassKey. StudentID and Name are obvious, and are obtained from the University's central records, as are Program and Plans. The notation

3978/1
indicates program 3978 and stage 1

Most of the remaining fields are marks. The following convention is used when assignments are submitted using the give system:

ValueMeaning
. No mark entered. No record of submission.
? No mark entered, but submission noted.
markCurrent mark value.

A mark value may be followed by a single letter. The letter is a code signifying some additional attribute of the mark, and doesn't affect its value. Commonly used codes are

L submission was late
E exemption from this assessment
C mark adjusted due to copying
P same as above; plagiarism
X artificially calculated mark

Rank field has this format n /total or n=/total n= means there is at least one other student with the same rank.
